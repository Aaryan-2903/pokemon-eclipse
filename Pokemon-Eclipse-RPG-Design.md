## Main Story

# Pokémon Eclipse — Main Story

## Prologue

Many years ago, an event known as **The Great Eclipse** occurred in the Eclipse Region.

For a few minutes, the sun disappeared completely.

When the light returned, strange crystals known as **Eclipse Shards** appeared across the region.

These shards emitted a mysterious energy never seen before.

Over time, people forgot about the incident.

But the Pokémon never did.

---

## Chapter 1 — The Beginning

The player lives in **Eclipse Town**.

Professor Nova, a renowned Pokémon researcher, is studying unusual Pokémon behavior near ancient Eclipse Shard sites.

The player receives a starter Pokémon from Professor Nova.

At the same time, the player's childhood rival **Kai** receives another starter.

Both begin their journeys to investigate the region.

---

## Chapter 2 — The Strange Pokémon

As the player travels through Route 1 and the nearby forest, wild Pokémon begin acting aggressively.

Some Pokémon possess a strange dark aura.

Professor Nova believes the Eclipse Shards are becoming active again.

Reports arrive from towns across the region.

The phenomenon is spreading.

---

## Chapter 3 — Team Umbra

A mysterious organization known as **Team Umbra** emerges.

Unlike ordinary villains, they do not seek money or power.

They believe humanity has weakened Pokémon by forcing coexistence.

Their goal is to awaken the legendary force sleeping beneath the Eclipse Region and create a world ruled entirely by powerful Pokémon.

Their leader is known only as **The Oracle**.

Nobody knows their true identity.

---

## Chapter 4 — The Lost Civilization

While collecting badges, the player discovers ancient ruins hidden throughout the region.

The ruins reveal a forgotten civilization.

Thousands of years ago, people used Eclipse Energy as a power source.

Eventually they attempted to control a legendary Pokémon known as:

### Eclipseon

The Guardian of Light and Shadow.

The experiment failed.

The civilization vanished.

Their cities were destroyed.

Their warning was buried in the ruins.

---

## Chapter 5 — The Rival's Fall

Kai becomes obsessed with becoming stronger.

After repeated defeats, Kai begins secretly studying Eclipse Energy.

Believing strength is everything, Kai starts using unstable Eclipse Shards during battles.

His Pokémon become stronger.

But their behavior becomes increasingly dangerous.

The friendship between the player and Kai begins to fracture.

---

## Chapter 6 — The Awakening

Team Umbra gathers Eclipse Shards from across the region.

At the ancient Eclipse Temple, they perform a ritual.

The ritual awakens Eclipseon.

The balance between light and darkness collapses.

Strange weather appears.

Wild Pokémon become uncontrollable.

Entire routes are altered by Eclipse Energy.

---

## Chapter 7 — The Truth

The player learns a shocking secret.

The Oracle is actually Professor Nova's former research partner.

Years ago, the two discovered the truth behind Eclipse Energy.

Nova wanted to protect the region.

The Oracle wanted to use the power.

Their friendship ended.

Now their conflict threatens the entire region.

---

## Chapter 8 — The Eclipse Battle

The player, Kai, and Professor Nova travel to the Eclipse Temple.

Kai finally realizes his mistakes.

Together they battle Team Umbra.

After defeating the Oracle, Eclipseon remains unstable.

The player must battle Eclipseon.

During the battle, Eclipseon tests the player's heart.

The player can either capture Eclipseon or restore its power and allow it to remain free.

---

## Chapter 9 — Pokémon League

With balance restored, the region begins to recover.

The player challenges the Elite Four.

After defeating them, the player faces the Champion.

Upon victory, the player becomes the first trainer recognized as:

### The Eclipse Champion

---

## Post-Game

New areas unlock.

Rare Pokémon appear.

Secret ruins become accessible.

The player discovers evidence that Eclipseon may not be the only legendary Pokémon connected to the Great Eclipse.

A second legendary entity watches from the shadows.

The story is not over.
